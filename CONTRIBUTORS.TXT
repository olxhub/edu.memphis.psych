Contributors
============

Course
======

The course team is Mollie Blair Anderson, Randy Floyd, Emily N
Srisarajivakul, and Kara Watkins, who are the authors of the source
content.

Adaptations of the content to a digital format was initially performed
principally by Paul Deane, with later adaptations by Piotr Mitros.

Platform
========

This platform, as well as the Learning Observer project it is part of,
and the Open edX platform they are based on, were originally conceived
of and created by Piotr Mitros.

This work has been in close collaboration with Paul Deane, who was a
constant thought partner throughout this project. He created
educational content, pedagogical ideas, and system prototypes which
were expanded upon, developed, and integrated into this system.

The core developer and architect of the Learning Observer for many
years was Brad Erickson. He made core contributions to lo-blocks,
primarily when it was still in the main Learning Observer repository.

The system was developed in close collaboration with Collin Lynch of
NCSU, and his team of graduate and undergraduate students. We will
include more about their own individual contributions at some point.

The educational content (present under `/content/` at the time of this
writing, moving into its own repository in the future) came from
course teams named there-in, especially ones at Memphis and at Georgia
State, under the leadership of John Sabatini. Without direct code
contributions, this course codesign helped significantly improve the
platform.

Portions of the code in this platform are automatically translated
versions of code I developed for the Open edX platform, often later
modernized by others in that platform, and by myself after translation
to JavaScript. Open edX is based on LON-CAPA by Gerd Kortemeyer.

The seed funding for this project came from Schmidt Futures, with
continued funding for development through ETS allocation funding, IES
grant R305A210297, and IES grant R305T240021.
